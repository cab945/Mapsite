Photosphere 10 - Souhane, Algeria

This photosphere looks from the Atlas Mountains in Northern Algeria, over the arid mountains of Algeria and the fertile plains of northern Algeria, as well as the capital city, Algeirs. This photosphere is very high quality and in my opinion has a great view of this part of Algeria. One downside is that most of the photosphere is just the mountains, although it does detail their arid slopes in high quality.
