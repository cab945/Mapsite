Photosphere 9 - Botlikh, Dagestan, Russia

This photosphere in the beautiful Republic of Dagestan, in the Caucausus region of Russia, really has an amazing view. In basically all directions you can see for miles, with near no obstructions except the elegant mountains. Directly in front of you are the elegant slopes of a mountain, which lies north of the town of Botlikh and just east of the border with Chechnya.

Photosphere 10 - Souhane, Algeria

This photosphere looks from the Atlas Mountains in Northern Algeria, over the arid mountains of Algeria and the fertile plains of northern Algeria, as well as the capital city, Algeirs. This photosphere is very high quality and in my opinion has a great view of this part of Algeria.

Photosphere 10 - Handa Island, Scotland

This photosphere on the northern Scottish island of Handa includes lush green plains and rocky, steep cliffs. In the distance, you can see the hills of the Scottish highlands on the mainland. There are also some people sitting and standing on the cliff edge in this photosphere.
